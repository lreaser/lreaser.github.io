Tutors

     Medha Gupta is a TJ alumni that graduated in 2020. She competed on the Congressional Debate team for four years and was the captain in 2020. Medha participated locally and nationally extensively, winning 3 bids to the Tournament of Champions. Medha has competed in both Forensics and Congress Debate and has been coaching TJ students in speech and debate since 2020 Particular areas of expertise include rebuttal speeches, constructive speeches, rhetorical and statistical analysis, and introduction to Congressional Debate and extemporaneous speaking. All lessons are free If interested, please contact Medha at medha.gupta@nyu.edu

     Dany Matar is a TJ alumni that graduated in 2019. He was a 4-year member and 2-year captain of the TJ Congressional Debate team. Dany participated locally and nationally extensively, winning 15 bids to the Tournament of Champions. Dany has competed in both Forensics and Congress Debate and has been coaching TJ students in speech and debate since 2019. Particular areas of expertise include rebuttal speeches, constructive speeches, rhetorical and statistical analysis, and introduction to Congressional Debate and extemporaneous speaking. First trial lesson is free and any proceeding lesson prices are negotiable. If interested, please contact Dany at danymatar1@gmail.com
